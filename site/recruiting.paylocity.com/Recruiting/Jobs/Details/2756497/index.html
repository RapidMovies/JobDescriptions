Full Stack Developer Intern

We are seeking talented and motivated sophomores, juniors, or seniors pursuing a Computer Science or Computer Engineering degree to join our team as interns. This internship will provide you with valuable hands-on experience in a dynamic and innovative environment. Successful candidates will work with Cryptic Vector engineers to design, implement and test software solutions for the United States government. This position will give you the opportunity to collaborate with an engineering team to accomplish the problems using state-of-the-art innovations and technologies.

Responsibilities

  • Contribute to the design and development of front-end user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript/TypeScript.
  • Work with APIs to integrate different systems and services.
  • Develop mission-critical software and automation solutions.
  • Explore containerization technologies and Kubernetes for efficient deployment and management of applications.
  • Learn and apply a variety of other technologies and skills as needed.

Requirements

Qualifications:
  • Enrolled in a Computer Science or Computer Engineering degree program.
  • Strong academic record with a focus on relevant coursework.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, Java, or Go (prior experience not required).
  • A passion for technology and a desire to build innovative products.
  • Ability to learn quickly and adapt to new technologies and challenges.
  • US Citizenship is required.
  • 40 hours per week onsite at Cryptic Vector.

This role requires use of technical data subject to U.S. Government contract restrictions, therefore this posting is only for U.S. Citizens.
