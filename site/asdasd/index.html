Responsibilities

  • Assist in the architecture design and development of our Multi-Cloud CDN platform.
  • Support the optimization and continuous improvement of systems for configuration management, data analysis, content management, and scheduling.
  • Participate in developing tools and solutions to enhance business access efficiency and optimize TikTok's global traffic management.
  • Collaborate with senior engineers to implement flexible and configurable scheduling strategies.
  • Contribute to the maintenance and troubleshooting of the Multi-Cloud CDN platform to ensure smooth operation.
  • Learn and apply best practices for Multi-Cloud CDN development and optimization.

Qualifications

Minimum Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree or above in computer science, electronics and communication, mathematics, physics, or related fields.
  • Familiarity with one or two programming languages such as C/C++, Go, Python, Java, or PHP in a Linux environment.
  • Strong communication skills, execution ability, and a willingness to learn and work collaboratively in a team environment.

Preferred Qualifications
  • Basic technical foundation with some exposure to API/platform design and a fundamental understanding of network protocols, storage, and message queues.
  • Basic understanding of CDN working principles, with an interest in gaining experience in CDN system design and development.
