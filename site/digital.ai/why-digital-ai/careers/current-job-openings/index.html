About the Role

We are searching for an intern who is studying Computer Science or Software Engineering to join our Shared Services team. This team builds a services ecosystem in support of the entire product suite at Digital.ai, including web services such as our Identity Service and reusable React components for our component library.

Whether you bring strengths in databases, web services, web frontends, or all of the above, you will join a small cross-functional development team that works on the whole stack.

As an intern on this team, you will:

  • Participate as a member of the team to design, develop, document, and test shared services and common UI components
  • Contribute to features and stories while maintaining a collaborative work environment
  • Have a continuous improvement mindset

Tools We Use

Although experience in these technologies is a plus, candidates should consider these to be the learning opportunities provided by the internship rather than requirements:

  • Python 3, Flask
  • Postgres, MongoDB
  • Kubernetes, Github Actions, Argo CD
